/ ADU FOR RENTAL INCOME

Ready to increase your income with an ADU rental?

The Pinellas rental market is hot. A well-designed ADU can rent for $1,800–$2,800/month, turning unused yard space into a passive income stream that funds itself.

A custom-built ADU also adds 20–35% to your home's appraised value, making it one of the highest-ROI improvements you can make.

/ ADU FOR AGING PARENTS

Why an ADU for your aging parents?

As parents age, their comfort, safety, and independence become a priority. Assisted living is costly. Keeping them in your home may lack space and privacy.

A custom-built ADU offers the perfect blend of closeness, independence, and security for them and for you.

/ ADU FOR ADULT CHILDREN

Struggling to find affordable housing for your adult children?

Soaring housing costs have made it harder than ever for young adults to gain independence, especially in the Tampa Bay market.

A custom-built ADU provides the ideal solution: an affordable, comfortable, and private home that keeps them close to family support.

FAQs

By far, the most common placement is in the backyard. There are some exceptions like sideyard or even front-of-house, provided there's enough room when considering setbacks, local Pinellas regulations, and any unique easements on your property. Our free property analysis tells you exactly what's possible.
Because our ADUs are built on-site from concrete block, every one is fully custom. Floor plans, room sizes, kitchen fixtures, finishes are all up to you. Most Pinellas municipalities require the exterior to match your primary home for permitting.
Every Thor ADU comes with a full kitchen (quartz counters, shaker cabinets, stainless appliances), bathroom (fiberglass shower, tile), luxury vinyl flooring, mini-split HVAC, tankless water heater, in-unit washer/dryer, and Florida-code hurricane construction.
Many financing options work for ADU projects. The most common is a Home Equity Line Of Credit (HELOC), but renovation loans and personal loans are also viable. We can point you toward lenders who specialize in ADU financing once you have an estimate in hand.
All Thor ADUs are built on your property from concrete block, the same construction we use for primary homes in Florida. Concrete block is engineered for hurricane resistance, lasts longer than wood-frame, and adds more value to your property compared to prefab. No craning, no transport fees, just solid construction from the ground up.
Yes. All Thor ADUs are built on a permanent slab foundation as standard, but raised foundations are available for properties in flood zones or on uneven lots.
Depending on your municipality, the largest ADU you can build is typically 1,000 sq ft. Pinellas cities (St. Pete, Clearwater, Largo, Seminole, etc.) each have their own rules. Contact us today or check with your local planning department.
Permitting is the most variable phase, typically 2 to 4 months in Pinellas County. Once permits are issued, the build runs 4 to 6 months depending on the model. Most homeowners are looking at 8 to 12 months from contract to keys.
Yes, all ADUs must be permitted, including conversions of existing space. We handle this entire process for you, making it as easy and stress-free as possible.
Most ADUs start around 384 sq ft. With standard setbacks, you typically need about 25' x 30' of clear backyard space to build an ADU. Larger lots open up bigger options, and we'll design fully custom around whatever space you have.
Our pricing covers foundation, construction, finishes, design, engineering, and permit submission. The only items not included are city/county permit fees and utility hookup fees, both of which we estimate clearly upfront during your property analysis.
An ADU (Accessory Dwelling Unit) is a self-contained living space with a full kitchen and bathroom, officially approved through your city or county's permitting process.
